Blue Chip Swing Trader™

This Privacy Policy is incorporated into, and subject to, our Terms of Service, including its arbitration, governing law, and limitation of liability provisions. See also our User Manual.

1. What We Collect

When you sign in, we collect your email address (used for passwordless magic-link sign-in via Supabase — we never see or store a password). When you subscribe, Stripe collects your payment details directly; we never receive or store your card number. We store only your Stripe customer ID, subscription ID, plan tier, subscription status, and trial dates, so the app can check your entitlement. To prevent free-trial abuse, we also store a one-way card "fingerprint" provided by Stripe — a non-reversible identifier for the card used, not the card number itself. We retain this card fingerprint only for as long as necessary to detect free-trial abuse, and it cannot be used to derive your actual card number.

2. What Stays in Your Browser Only

Your watchlist, your entered positions, and your Anthropic API key (for the optional AI Bracket feature) are stored only in your own browser's local storage. None of this is ever transmitted to or stored on our servers.

3. AI Features

AI Analysis sends your current qualifying stock list (tickers and scores only — no personal data) to Anthropic's Claude API for commentary. AI Bracket uses your own Anthropic API key to send chart images and stock data directly from your browser to Anthropic — this never passes through our servers at all, and we have no visibility into it.

BCST does not control, and is not responsible for, how Anthropic processes, retains, or uses data submitted to its API. Anthropic's handling of this data is governed by Anthropic's own privacy policy and terms, available at anthropic.com. For AI Bracket specifically, because the request goes directly from your browser to Anthropic using your own API key, BCST has no technical visibility into, log of, or ability to delete that data on your behalf.

4. Who We Share Data With

  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription billing
  • Supabase — authentication and account/subscription data storage
  • Resend — sending transactional email (e.g. a welcome email after signup)
  • Anthropic — AI Analysis and AI Bracket commentary (see Section 3)
  • Yahoo Finance — source of public market data displayed in the app (no personal data is sent)

Each of these providers processes data under its own privacy policy; we encourage you to review Stripe's, Supabase's, Resend's, and Anthropic's privacy practices directly.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use advertising trackers.

5. Cookies & Sessions

We use a session token (via Supabase) stored in your browser to keep you signed in. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures (including encrypted connections and reputable third-party infrastructure providers) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your device and any locally stored data (including your Anthropic API key, if used) secure.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain account and subscription data for as long as your account is active. After account closure, we retain billing-related records for seven (7) years to comply with tax and accounting obligations, and delete or anonymize other account data within ninety (90) days of your deletion request, except where retention is required by law.

To request deletion of your account and associated data, email support@bluechipswingtrader.com.
