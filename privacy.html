Privacy Policy

Blue Chip Swing Trader™ — see also our Terms of Service

1. What We Collect

When you sign in, we collect your email address (used for passwordless magic-link sign-in via Supabase — we never see or store a password). When you subscribe, Stripe collects your payment details directly; we never receive or store your card number. We store only your Stripe customer ID, subscription ID, plan tier, subscription status, and trial dates, so the app can check your entitlement. To prevent free-trial abuse, we also store a one-way card "fingerprint" provided by Stripe — a non-reversible identifier for the card used, not the card number itself.

2. What Stays in Your Browser Only

Your watchlist, your entered positions, and your Anthropic API key (for the optional AI Bracket feature) are stored only in your own browser's local storage. None of this is ever transmitted to or stored on our servers.

3. AI Features

AI Analysis sends your current qualifying stock list (tickers and scores only — no personal data) to Anthropic's Claude API for commentary. AI Bracket uses your own Anthropic API key to send chart images and stock data directly from your browser to Anthropic — this never passes through our servers at all, and we have no visibility into it.

4. Who We Share Data With

  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription billing
  • Supabase — authentication and account/subscription data storage
  • Resend — sending transactional email (e.g. a welcome email after signup)
  • Anthropic — AI Analysis and AI Bracket commentary (see Section 3)
  • Yahoo Finance — source of public market data displayed in the app (no personal data is sent)

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use advertising trackers.

5. Cookies & Sessions

We use a session token (via Supabase) stored in your browser to keep you signed in. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain account and subscription data for as long as your account is active, plus a reasonable period afterward for billing records. To request deletion of your account and associated data, email support@bluechipswingtrader.com.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at, and should not be used by, anyone under 18 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to support@bluechipswingtrader.com.
